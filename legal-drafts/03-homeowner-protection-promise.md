## Our promise to you

When you book a service professional through Freddy FixIt, we want you to feel confident. That's why we stand behind the experience with our Homeowner Protection Promise.

**What we do up front:**

- We bring on **top-quality** home and vehicle service professionals.
- Every Contractor is **screened**, and we require them to provide the documents and licences relevant to their trade before they can take jobs.
- If something isn't right, you can **submit a claim**, and if it meets our eligibility requirements, we'll take appropriate action to help resolve the situation.
- We provide **ongoing support** through our help chat and our team of customer support experts.

## Important: what this Promise is and isn't

The Homeowner Protection Promise is a **goodwill satisfaction program offered at Freddy FixIt's discretion**. It is **not insurance**, not a warranty on a Contractor's work, and not a guarantee of any specific payment or outcome. It does not replace any rights you have directly against a Contractor or under applicable law. Contractors are independent businesses responsible for their own work; this Promise reflects our commitment to help make things right within the limits described here.

## What may be eligible

You may be eligible to submit a claim if, for a job booked and managed through the Platform:

- the Contractor did not perform the agreed work, or performed it materially differently from what was agreed; or
- the completed work was clearly defective or not done to a reasonable professional standard; or
- the Contractor caused property damage in the course of the work through their fault.

## Eligibility requirements

To be considered, a claim generally must meet all of the following:

1. The job was **booked and paid (where applicable) through the Freddy FixIt Platform** — not arranged privately or off-Platform.
2. Your **account is in good standing** and you have complied with these Terms and the User Agreement.
3. You **report the issue promptly** — within **14 days** of the work being completed or scheduled.
4. You provide the information we reasonably need (see "How to submit a claim").
5. You give the Contractor and/or Freddy FixIt a **reasonable opportunity to inspect and remedy** the issue before seeking other resolution.
6. The issue is not within an excluded category below.

## What is not covered

This Promise does not apply to, among other things:

- normal wear and tear, pre-existing conditions, or issues not caused by the Contractor's work;
- work, materials, or changes you arranged directly with the Contractor outside the Platform;
- consequential, indirect, or incidental losses (for example, lost time, lost income, or inconvenience);
- damage caused by your own actions, by third parties, or by circumstances outside the Contractor's control;
- cosmetic preferences or changes of mind where the work matches what was agreed;
- claims you do not report within the eligibility window or for which you cannot provide reasonable supporting information.

## How to submit a claim

1. Contact us through the **in-app help chat** or at **hello@freddyfixit.ca** as soon as you notice the issue.
2. Tell us your name, the job, the Contractor, and what went wrong.
3. Provide supporting details — photos, dates, the agreed scope and price, and any communication with the Contractor.
4. Allow us and the Contractor reasonable access to assess the situation.

## How we resolve claims

Our support team reviews each eligible claim individually and works in good faith to find a fair resolution. Depending on the situation, appropriate action **may** include arranging for the Contractor to correct the work, coordinating another qualified Contractor, mediating between you and the Contractor, or another remedy we consider reasonable. **The resolution, and whether a claim qualifies, are determined by Freddy FixIt at its reasonable discretion**, and any specific dollar amounts, caps, or remedies will be communicated as part of the claim — this Promise does not commit us to a fixed payout.

## Ongoing support

Whether or not a claim applies, our help chat and customer support experts are here to help you through your experience on the Platform.

## Changes

We may update or discontinue the Homeowner Protection Promise at any time. The version in effect when your job was booked applies to that job. The current version is always posted here with its "Last updated" date.
