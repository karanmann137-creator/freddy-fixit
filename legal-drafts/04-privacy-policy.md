## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Freddy FixIt collects, uses, discloses, and protects personal information when you use our website, applications, and services (the "Platform"). We handle personal information in accordance with Alberta's **Personal Information Protection Act (PIPA)** and, where it applies, Canada's **Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA)**.

By using the Platform, you consent to the practices described here.

## 2. Personal information we collect

Depending on how you use the Platform, we may collect:

- **Account information:** name, email address, phone number, password (stored in hashed form by our authentication provider), and your role (customer or contractor).
- **If you sign in with Google:** basic profile information Google shares with us, such as your name and email, in accordance with your Google permissions.
- **Service request information:** the details you provide about a job — service needed, description, location/address, scheduling preferences, and photos you upload.
- **Contractor information (for service professionals):** business and company details, service areas, specialties, years of experience, licences and certifications, insurance and WCB/WorkSafe information, government identification, references, and portfolio photos.
- **Reviews and communications:** ratings, reviews, and messages you send through or to the Platform and our support channels.
- **Payment-related information:** where payments are processed, limited transaction details. Card and bank details are handled by our payment processor, not stored by us. *(Update this section when in-Platform payments go live.)*
- **Technical information:** basic information automatically collected when you use the Platform, such as device and browser type and log data, used to operate and secure the service.

## 3. How we use personal information

We use personal information to:

- create and manage your account and authenticate sign-in;
- match customers with contractors and facilitate quotes, bookings, scheduling, and job completion;
- screen and verify contractors and their credentials;
- process payments and applicable fees (where enabled);
- send service-related communications and notifications (for example, status updates, confirmations, and password resets);
- provide customer support through our help chat and support team;
- administer the Homeowner Protection Promise and handle claims;
- maintain safety, prevent fraud and misuse, and enforce our terms;
- improve and develop the Platform; and
- comply with legal obligations.

We collect, use, and disclose personal information only for purposes that a reasonable person would consider appropriate in the circumstances, and we limit collection to what is necessary for these purposes.

## 4. Consent

We obtain your consent to collect, use, and disclose your personal information, which may be express or implied depending on the sensitivity of the information and the circumstances. You may withdraw consent (subject to legal or contractual restrictions) by contacting us, though doing so may limit our ability to provide the Platform to you.

## 5. Disclosure and service providers

We share personal information only as needed to operate the Platform:

- **Between customers and contractors:** to facilitate a job, we share the information each side needs (for example, a customer's job details and address with a matched contractor, and a contractor's profile and contact details with the customer).
- **With service providers ("processors")** who help us run the Platform, including:
  - **Supabase** — database, authentication, file storage, and backend hosting;
  - **Vercel** — website/application hosting;
  - **Resend** — transactional email delivery;
  - **Cloudflare** — DNS and network services;
  - **Google** — sign-in (where you choose Google sign-in);
  - a **payment processor** — where in-Platform payments are enabled.
- **For legal reasons:** where required by law, regulation, legal process, or to protect rights, safety, and security.
- **Business transfers:** in connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, subject to appropriate protections.

Our service providers may store or process data outside Alberta or Canada (for example, in the United States). Where that occurs, the information is subject to the laws of those jurisdictions, and we take reasonable steps to ensure appropriate protection. You may contact us for information about our data-handling practices and the providers involved.

We do not sell your personal information.

## 6. Safeguards and retention

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect personal information against loss, theft, and unauthorized access, including access controls and encryption in transit. No system is perfectly secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

We retain personal information only as long as necessary for the purposes described here or as required by law, after which we take reasonable steps to delete or anonymize it. You can request deletion of your account, and we provide self-serve account deletion within the Platform.

## 7. Your rights

Subject to applicable law, you may:

- **access** the personal information we hold about you and request information about how it has been used and disclosed;
- request **correction** of inaccurate or incomplete information;
- **withdraw consent** or request deletion of your account and associated personal information;
- ask questions or raise concerns about our handling of your information.

To exercise these rights, contact us at **hello@freddyfixit.ca**. We will respond within the timeframes required by applicable law. If you are not satisfied with our response, you may contact the **Office of the Information and Privacy Commissioner of Alberta** or, where PIPEDA applies, the **Office of the Privacy Commissioner of Canada**.

## 8. Children

The Platform is intended for adults (18+) and is not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## 9. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated version here with a new "Last updated" date and, where appropriate, provide additional notice. Your continued use of the Platform after changes take effect means you accept the updated Policy.
