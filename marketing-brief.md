## Positioning statement

**For** Calgary homeowners who need a repair done and don't want to gamble on a stranger,
**Freddy Fix It** is the home-services marketplace **that** delivers up to 3 fixed-price quotes from actively vetted local pros and protects your payment until the job's done right —
**unlike** directories like HomeStars or Jiffy that hand you a list of names and leave the vetting, pricing, and risk to you.

## One-liner

Post a job, get up to **3 fixed-price quotes** from **vetted, licensed Calgary pros**, and only pay when the work is done right.

## Who we serve

- **Homeowners** in Calgary who need home repairs or maintenance and don't want to chase quotes, vet strangers, or risk getting burned. *(Primary demand-side audience — lead with this.)*
- **Vehicle owners** who want trustworthy maintenance (oil changes, tires, brakes) without the dealership runaround.
- **Local tradespeople / contractors** looking for a steady stream of qualified, ready-to-book jobs. *(Supply side — recruit enough pros to keep quote turnaround fast.)*

### Primary persona — "Cautious Calgary Homeowner"

- 30–60, owns a single-family home or townhouse in Calgary, household income mid-to-upper.
- Trigger moments: something breaks (furnace, leak, roof after hail), a season flips (winter prep, spring cleanup), or they're tackling a deferred to-do list.
- Pain: doesn't know who to trust, hates phone tag, fears overpaying or a no-show, no recourse if it goes wrong.
- What wins them: clear price up front, proof the pro is vetted, and a no-commitment way to see the cost. **Trust beats speed.**

## The problem we solve

Finding a trustworthy local pro is slow and stressful: endless phone tag, vague pricing, no-shows, and no recourse if the job goes sideways. Freddy Fix It removes the guesswork and the risk.

## Core value propositions

1. **Vetted pros only.** Every contractor is screened — licensed, insured, WCB-covered, with references checked. We do the background work so the homeowner doesn't have to.
2. **Up to 3 fixed-price quotes.** No hourly surprises. Post once, compare clear prices, pick your pro.
3. **Homeowner Protection Promise.** Payment is held safely and only released when the client confirms the work is done. If something's wrong, there's a built-in dispute process.
4. **Real-time job tracking.** "On my way" updates, a live job timeline, and instant email + in-app notifications keep clients in the loop.
5. **Honest reviews.** Public, verified contractor reviews on every profile — real feedback from real Calgary jobs.
6. **Low-friction start.** Get a free quote before you even sign up. No commitment to see what your job costs.
7. **Local, Calgary-first.** Built for Calgary homeowners and trades — not a faceless national directory.

## Goals & KPIs (set targets per quarter)

Marketing exists to grow **demand** (job posts) without starving **supply** (available pros). Track both.

- **North-star:** completed, client-confirmed jobs per month.
- **Acquisition:** new free-quote requests; signups; cost per quote request.
- **Activation:** % of quote requests that become posted jobs; quote-turnaround time.
- **Retention:** repeat-booking rate (lean on the repeat-customer perk); reviews collected.
- **Supply health:** active vetted contractors; avg # quotes per request (target: 3).
- **SEO:** ranking blog posts, organic sessions, organic → quote conversion.

## Conversion funnel (homeowner)

See ad / blog / social → land on a cost or service page → **"Get a free quote"** (primary CTA, pre-signup) → receive up to 3 quotes → pick a pro → job tracked to completion → confirm + review → repeat booking.
Every campaign should name which funnel stage it serves and point to the free-quote CTA.

## Claims & compliance (READ BEFORE PUBLISHING)

- **Payment protection is LIVE (as of 2026-06-26).** Stripe Connect is built and running on live keys. The client pays through the site, the money is **held**, and it's only released to the contractor after the client confirms the work is done (or after a 3-day auto-confirm). You may now make present-tense protection claims: "your payment is held until you confirm the work," "you don't pay until the job's done right," "money's protected while we review a dispute." Keep wording to **"payment held / protected until you confirm"** rather than the legal term "escrow," and don't imply it's a bank/insurance product — it's a platform-held payment with a dispute process. The Homeowner Protection Promise itself is a discretionary, claims-based satisfaction program (not insurance) — describe it that way.
- **Vetting claims must be true per pro.** Only say "licensed / insured / WCB-covered / reference-checked" where the contractor's vetting answers are on file. Avoid blanket guarantees you can't back per job.
- **Reviews:** only "verified" reviews tied to real completed jobs. No incentivized or fabricated reviews (Alberta / Competition Act).
- **Local + comparison claims** (vs HomeStars/Jiffy): keep to factual differences (fixed-price quotes, active vetting). Avoid disparaging unverifiable claims.
